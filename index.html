The Science

How Does It Work?

Ollas are an ancient irrigation technique. Our terracotta globes use the same principle of slow, steady osmosis to keep your plants perfectly hydrated.

1

Fill the Globe

Simply unscrew the cork top and fill your Azen Garden globe with water. The easy top-refill design means no mess, no fuss. Refill every few days or once a week depending on your plant's needs.

2

Insert into Soil

Push the globe into your planter's soil. The porous terracotta clay body makes direct contact with the root zone. The plant literally controls its own watering.

3

Roots Drink on Demand

Water is delivered precisely where it's needed, directly to the roots, reducing waste and eliminating overwatering.
