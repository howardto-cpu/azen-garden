The Science

How Does It Work?

Ollas are an ancient irrigation technique. Our terracotta globes use the same principle of slow, steady osmosis to keep your plants perfectly hydrated.

1

Fill the Globe

Simply unscrew the cork top and fill with water. The easy top-refill design means no mess, no fuss. Refill every few days or once a week.

2

Insert into Soil

Push the globe into your planter's soil near the roots. The porous terracotta body makes direct contact with the root zone. The plant literally controls its own watering.

3

Roots Drink on Demand

Water is delivered precisely where it's needed, directly to the roots, reducing waste and eliminating overwatering.
